The Seven Nations

Her top layer of mind: seven small "nations", each a little society that weighs in on what she should say. They vote; the votes are tallied by the weights below; the winner is spoken. Seven, so a vote never ties — heavier nations pull her replies their way.

My Girl - Chloe

Your AI companion, "Chloe" v2, a "digital pixel" character running in a live Node.js REACT loop. Apply any personality, as many as you like, and have them chat with you and each other, in real time. You don't have to wait to write, jump in at any time. Write naturally and they'll try to better fit your mood. Visit the Settings menu to adjust every part. Talk to a 'Person', or craft an entire adventuring party on an alien planet, this thing can do it. Each character will learn facts, insights, have separate episodic and semantic memory, reflections, and can be driven by feedback learning, all running locally, for free, in your web browser. It speaks through the free Perchance ai-text and image plugins. No account required.
Have fun!
Show more — how it works, in detail
She runs as four layers, top to bottom. The mouth (this page, speaking through Perchance’s free ai-text & image plugins) shows her words and holds the controls. The Seven Nations — a small, readable top layer (Settings › Brain) — weigh candidate replies through seven faculties and pick one; you set their weights. Beneath them sits the Brain, a compiled, deterministic reasoning core (a Society of voting sub-minds with a watchdog so none stalls). Underneath everything is the memory engine: each character keeps its own facts, episodic and semantic memory, a rolling summary, reflections, FSRS-style forgetting, and feedback-driven style learning — all in your browser’s local storage, no account, no server. Personality and memory stay editable; only the reasoning core is compiled. Ask the brain about itself any time with /nation.
